About Us

About us

Change360 Network (C360Network)is a non-profit organisation driven by a group of young progressive Nigerians with a strong excellence-oriented mindset and belief of a better Nigeria. Our mission is to help young Nigerians discover and develop their leadership potential through constant mentoring and leadership training with the aim to position them with the right platform to inculcate the good leadership spirit needed to move our nation forward.

Change360 Network came up in 2013 as a way of promoting good leadership in Nigerian universities and as a way of encouraging more young persons to take up leadership positions in order to produce leaders who are ready to take on roles and responsibilities needed to drive our nation in the right direction .
